# Site spec

Top down and left to right detailing of all pages. Subdivisions based on header
are all co-located and describe something vertically down the page but in a
different logical container

## Banner
Present on every page:
1. Name in the top left corner
    * Styled Bold and as tall as the nav bar
2. Nav Bar containing the following panes
    1. Home
    2. Demo Reel
    3. 3D work
    4. Pandy Series
    5. Code
    6. 2D Work
    7. Resume
    8. Contact
    * Styled such that current selected pane is highlighted, the rest are matte
        grey. The Nav bar itself takes up roughly the inner 3/5 of the
        horizontal width.
## Footer
Present on every page:
1. left block: "Contact: email@email.edu"
2. right block: \<linkedIN button\>\<instagram button\>

## 3d Work

### Banner 
1. Bold text: "3D work"
2. Subtext: "I work primarily in Maya but also love working in Blender and
   Unreal Engine!"

### Gallery 

1. Gallery of n different image cards, each card is broken down into:
    -image-
    -title-
    -description-
2. Whenever any card image is clicked it then pops open the piece of art along
   with a longer form description beneath its title on the right hand side. 
   Next to the image is an arrow button to navigate between the pieces as flows
   from the root gallery in slide deck format. Many of the art works are
   animated or videos, and begin to play on hover.
